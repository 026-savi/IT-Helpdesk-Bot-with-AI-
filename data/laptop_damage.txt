Laptop Damage Emergency Solution:

If your laptop is physically damaged, overheated, smoking, or exploded:

1. Immediately turn off the laptop
2. Disconnect charger and power supply
3. Do not touch damaged battery parts
4. Move away from flammable objects
5. Contact IT support immediately

Safety Warning:
- Do not attempt to restart the laptop
- Do not use damaged battery
- Physical damage may cause fire hazard

Next Steps:
- Submit emergency IT ticket
- Visit hardware support center
- Replace damaged components if required
